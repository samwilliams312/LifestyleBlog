Exercises

Throughout my early adulthood it was easy to stay in shape and maintain a physique even when my diet was not the healthiest. Those days are far gone, and as a busy young woman I often times find it difficult to find workouts that are both effective and (somewhat) enjoyable. I will share my trials and tribulations, in hopes that you may take away some ammusement and workouts that fit into your schedule, so you can fit into those skinny jeans.
